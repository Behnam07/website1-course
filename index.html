About Team 3

On slack we brainstormed the style and genre of the game together, we all came to an agreement on what style and theme we wanted. We planned out the main idea of the game on slack and made sure that we kept the adventure aspect to our game. We then thought of scenarios and hazards that a young child might be in and wrote them all down and tried to implement as many of them as we could into our game design.
